Setting permissions for your Modmail bot is very important. This will affect who has access to respond to tickets and view them.

### Permission Levels

**OWNER** - (absolute control over the bot) **ADMINISTRATOR** - (administrative powers such as setting activities) **MODERATOR -** (ability to block and view aliases) **SUPPORTER** - (access to core Modmail supporting functions) **REGULAR** - (most basic interactions such as help and about)

### Command

To set the permissions you want for a specified role you can use: **\[p]permissions add PERMISSION\_LEVEL \<mentionrole> OR \<roleID> EXMAPLE: \[p]permissions add MODERATOR 998174230869047995**
