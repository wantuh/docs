### Title

RED - **\[p]config set thread\_close\_title Ticket Closed** In the title you can also include an emoji if you wish.

### Response

YELLOW - **\[p]config set thread\_close\_response Thank your for opening a ticket. If you require any further assistance feel free to open another ticket!** In the response, aka the embed description, please be reminded they are limited to **4096 characters**.

### Footer

GREEN - **\[p]config set thread\_close\_footer Wantuh's Helpdesk**

In the footer you want to keep it short, you can keep it to your bots name, do not include emojis, emojis do not work in footers.
